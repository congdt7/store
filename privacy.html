Privacy Policy — Quiz Rush

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Quiz Rush ("the Game", "we") is a trivia mini game. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data we collect

  • TikTok account identifier — when you open the Game, we use TikTok Login to obtain a unique account identifier (open ID) so we can recognise you across sessions.
  • Display name — a nickname you may enter to appear on the leaderboard.
  • Gameplay data — scores, answer streaks, coins, and progress.
  • Optional email — only if you choose to link an email to back up your progress.
  • Device / anonymous identifier — a random ID used to save your progress if you play without linking an account.

2. How we use it

  • To authenticate you and save your game progress.
  • To display leaderboards and competitive results.
  • To show rewarded video ads that you choose to watch in exchange for in-game help.
  • To prevent cheating and abuse.

3. Third-party services

  • TikTok — for sign-in and rewarded video advertising.
  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Firestore) — to store leaderboard entries and cloud saves.

4. Data retention & deletion

We keep your gameplay data while your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at the address below.

5. Children

The Game is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Contact

Questions or data requests: 15i1dtcong@gmail.com
